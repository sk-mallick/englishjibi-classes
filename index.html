About Us

ENGLISHJIBI CLASSES

We are a dedicated English & Grammar Coaching Center providing quality offline education for students from Class 2 to Class 10.

Our Mission

Building Strong Foundations

Our mission is to empower young minds with the power of language. We focus on grammar precision, vocabulary building, and confident communication to ensure academic success in school and beyond.

"Language is the dress of thought. We help students dress their thoughts with clarity and confidence."

— ENGLISHJIBI Faculty

Founder & Lead Educator

CHIRANJIBI SIR

14+ Years Experience English Grammar Specialist

“CHIRANJIBI SIR founded ENGLISHJIBI Classes over 14 years ago with a vision to strengthen students’ command over English grammar and communication. With deep classroom experience and a student-focused teaching approach, he has guided learners from Classes 2 to 10 to achieve clarity, confidence, and academic excellence.”

His teaching emphasizes strong grammatical foundations, practical usage, and individual attention.
